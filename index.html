Welcome to Yu Garden Tea

Discover the soul of Chinese tea culture just steps away from the famous Yu Garden. Whether you're looking for a peaceful break, a guided tea tasting, or the perfect Shanghai gift, our tea shop offers an authentic and relaxing experience for travelers from around the world.
